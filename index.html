Mass Effect Timeline

  • 68,000 BC-Protheans achieve spaceflight. They discover Mass Effect technology and found a galactic civilization, centered around an ancient galactic structure called the Citadel. Over the years, the Protheans study primitive species such as the humans and the asari who they deem to have the 'potential' to eventually join their empire.
  • 48,000 BC-Prothean Empire destroyed when the Reapers arrive from dark space via the Citadel. Over the next several centuries, the Protheans are systematically eradicated. The Reapers return to dark space.
  • 500 BC-Asari and Salarians found the Citadel Council. The new galactic community makes contact with and eventually grants embassies to new species, which are the volus, Batarians, Elcor, Hanar and quarians.
  • 1 AD-The Rachni Wars begin. An intelligent hive minded race of sentient insects (the Rachni) are discovered and wage war with the galaxy.
    • 80 AD-Krogan uplifted by the Citadel Council to fight the Rachni. They end up being pivotal in the war, since they have the fortitude to attack the Rachni queens in their nests.
    • 300 AD-Rachni declared extinct. Krogan rewarded with a new world, where their population skyrockets.
  • 300-700 AD-Krogan expansion concerns the Citadel Council. Special Tactics and Reconnaisance Group(SpecTRe) founded in response.
  • 700 AD-Krogan Rebellions begin. Krogan expansion into territory claimed by the other races leads to tensions. Krogan refuse an ultimatum to withdraw from the Asari colony of Lusia, leading to a preemptive strike by the Spectres. Tensions explode into full scale war. Amidst the chaos, the Council contact the Turians for the first time, and succeeds in acquiring their aid for the current conflict.
    • 710 AD-Genophage engineered by the salarians due to the krogan's population growth rate. Used by the turians since they felt that the war would never be won otherwise. Krogan population finally declines.
    • 800 AD-Krogan Rebellions end, Citadel Conventions drawn up.
  • 900 AD-Turians accepted into the Citadel Council.
  • 1895 AD-Geth War begins. The geth, sentient machines created by the quarians for manual labor, rise up against their masters. Quarians driven from their worlds and are reduced to spacefaring nomads within the Migrant Fleet. The Council closes the Quarian embassy on the Citadel as a result.
  • 1921 AD-Geth begin construction of a superstructure to hold and run every geth program in existence. The completion of this is the geth's ultimate goal for their civilization.
  • 1926 AD-On Earth US scientist Robert Goddard (the "Father of Modern Rocketry") launches the first liquid-fueled rocket.
  • 1942 AD-Second World War rages on Earth. Germany successfully tests the V-2 rocket on 3 October 1942, the first ballistic missile.
  • 1961 AD-Soviet cosmonaut Yuri Gagarin aboard Vostok 1 becomes the first human to travel in space.
  • 1969 AD-US astronaut Neil Armstrong walks on the moon.
  • 2069 AD-Armstrong Outpost founded at Shackleton Crater. First human outpost on the moon.
  • 2103 AD-European Space Agency founds Lowell City on Mars.
  • 2148 AD-Humanity discovers Mass Effect technology within Prothean ruins found on Mars.
  • 2149 AD-Systems Alliance founded by 18 of Earth's largest nations.
  • 2157 AD-First Contact War begins. Humanity's first contact with a hostile alien race (the Turians). The Turians invade the human colony of Shanxi after an attempt to activate a dormant mass relay, a violation of Council law. The Alliance manages to lead the effort to drive the Turians from the colony. Represented humanity's first contact with the galactic community, after the Citadel Council stepped in to broker peace.
  • 2183 AD-Events of Mass Effect 1 occur. Commander Shepard becomes a prominent figure in the galaxy due to their actions.
  • 2185 AD-Events of Mass Effect 2 occur. The Andromeda Initiative, a multi-species civilian project intended to settle in the Andromeda galaxy launches that year.
  • 2186 AD-Events of Mass Effect 3 occur. The decisions made by Shepard end up affecting the Milky Way galaxy forever.
  • 2818 AD-The Nexus station reaches the Andromeda galaxy.
  • 2819 AD-The human Ark Hyperion arrives in Andromeda 14 months after the Nexus.
